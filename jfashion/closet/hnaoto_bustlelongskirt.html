Its base is an 100% breathable polyester weave perfect for shielding the Summer elements while maintaining j-gothic casual. Besides the gauze strip glaze decorating the righthand, my favorite feature is the adjustable bustle.

I received this directly from a pop-up, and met Naoto at the table upon purchase. His staff was excited to talk about how he handstitches every piece on display, so of course knowing this made me feel more drawn to this skirt.

Best paired with loose tops and cardigans.
