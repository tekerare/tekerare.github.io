This hat is now discontinued, which sucks because its one of the best replicas I've seen of the original animated hat.

ANGELtype started off as an LA-based otaku fashion brand in 2020 and has since matured into something in the likes of contemporary minimalist styles like Yohji Yamamoto (all of the anime stuff was pretty much fazed out by 2024.)

For an otaku brand ANGELtype was the best above all clothing labels that started off on Instagram, no contest. They made bootlegs seem high-end; their mohair Nana sweater and NGE jewelry comes to mind. Just like SEL and other mentions they also created garments using the likeness of Devilman Crybaby, and Beserk.
