FunnyBunny: Pomni x Jax

I actually didn't think much of the pilot when it first came out. It teetered on an Invader Zim level of random humor that was comfortable enough to watch until followed by a few millenial-isms.

The pilot didn't do much to explore Pomni as a person as it stands to tell the workings (and inescapable fate) of the Digital Circus's macrocosm. What eventually solidified her as my favorite character was her assertion, emotional intelligence, and playful side displayed throughout the series, and it was grounded in mostly her interactions with Jax.

She openly explores the depths of her circus neighbors, showing gratitude to Ragatha's (kinda hopeless) efforts, receives advice from Kinger when least expected, and empathizes with Gangle as well as standing up for her when she's at her most stressed.

On the other hand, Jax is a jerk to everyone since frame one and is reserved by choice. His history with the rest of the cast spans into years, yet those who have been at TADC for longer than he has don't even seem to know much about his private/prior life or any of his avatar gimmicks (like what his avatar does when he holds his breath, why he breaks the fourth wall, etc.) He keeps it that way too, never getting closer to the other characters and actively antagonizing them.

Around August 2025, I went through and coped with a breakup. In the earlier periods of our relationship I struggled to trust my ex-girlfriend because of my previous relationships. Every time we had an argument she wouldn't let go of things she said she forgave me for, and I felt like I couldn't change because her resentment ruined my drive to get closer. I never felt safe to mess up around anyone because I knew she wouldn't trust me enough to forgive me.

Even though Pomni has seen all the nasty sides of Jax with others and in private, I felt comforted to see Pomni give Jax a chance and I feel comforted when she fights (literally) to preserve their relationship, even if it doesn't work out in the end. She didn't have to empathize with him, but she chose to and that's important to me. It also kind of comforts me to see someone as avoidant as Jax existing in media, so I guess I also like seeing him get beat up by Pomni. This ship helped sober me up and leave the past behind.

How I See Their Dynamic

Pomni and Jax both want to see themselves in each other. Jax seeks escapism (at the expense of others), and Pomni seeks company and a parallel that she can see herself in, whether it's a real human or an NPC.

What I really enjoy about FunnyBunny is that even if Jax doesn't always take Pomni seriously like he should, he still sees her as an equal and goes out of his way to involve her in his business, when he usually keeps others at armslength (and has for years). He shows her how to lose her realism because he wants to share his fun with her and he's an outlet for her from all the shock and hardships of being stuck in TADC.

Pomni's raw playfulness and shameless curiosity totally captured my heart. Even if she's on the timid side, she's never a doormat and often confronts or has advice to give to those who need it. She's the exact type of person who can handle Jax, and can keep Jax in his place. In return, he helps bring out her best stage of being and comfort and lets her be "evil".
