What Is FunnyBunny (Pomni x Jax)

I actually didn't think much of the pilot when it first came out. The humor just wasn't my speed; it teetered inbetween an Invader Zim level of "random" humor (which I like) that was comfortable enough to watch, until followed by a few millenial-isms (which I don't like). Not too demanding of a watch, so I just shrugged off the project and was into other things.

The pilot didn't do much to explore Pomni as a person as it mostly serves to tell the workings + inescapable fate of the Digital Circus's macrocosm. Instead what eventually solidified her as my favorite character was her assertion, emotional intelligence, and playful side displayed throughout the series, and it was grounded in mostly her interactions with Jax.

She doesn't hide exploring the depths of her circus neighbors; she shows gratitude to Ragatha's (kinda hopeless) efforts, receives advice from Kinger when least expected, and empathizes with Gangle as well as standing up for her when she's at her most stressed.

On the other hand, Jax is a jerk to everyone since frame one and is reserved by choice. His history with the rest of the cast spans into years, yet those who have been at TADC for longer than he has don't even seem to know much about his private/prior life or any of his avatar gimmicks (like what his avatar does when he holds his breath, why he breaks the fourth wall, etc.) He keeps it that way too, never getting closer to the other characters and actively antagonizing them.

Around August 2025, I went through and coped with a breakup. When we had an argument I felt like I couldn't change because the resentment for me ruined my drive to get closer.

Even though Pomni has seen all the nasty sides of Jax with others and in private, I felt comforted to see Pomni give Jax a chance and I feel comforted when she fights (literally) to preserve their relationship, even if it doesn't work out in the end. She didn't have to empathize with him, but she chose to and that's important to me. It also kind of comforts me to see someone as avoidant as Jax existing in media. This pairing helped sober me up and leave the past behind.

How I See Their Dynamic

Pomni and Jax both want to see themselves in each other. Jax seeks escapism (at the expense of others), and Pomni seeks company and a parallel that she can see herself in, whether it's a real human or an NPC.

What I really enjoy about FunnyBunny is that even if Jax doesn't always take Pomni seriously like he should, he still sees her as an equal and goes out of his way to involve her in his business, when he usually keeps others at armslength (and has for years). Episode 6 for example!

He could've just made fun of her or made her feel lame for considering pacifism during a gun brawl, but he doesn't. He shows her how to lose her realism, wanting to share his fun with her. He's become an outlet for her from all the shock and hardships of being stuck in TADC, and is the first person to make her laugh after maybe a week or two in the circus.

Pomni's raw playfulness and shameless curiosity totally captured my heart. Even if she's on the timid side, she's never a doormat and often confronts or has advice to give to those who need it. She's the exact type of person who can handle Jax, and seems to keep Jax in his place. As her equal, he helps bring out her most comfortable self and lets her be "evil".

(04:00) Ep 5: Untitled

"I thought a position of power suited you."

My favorite fanworks are those where Pomni has more life experience than Jax while he's timid in uncharted territory. That guy can't hold a serious or emotional conversation whatsoever. In any scenario where he actually decides to take something seriously, I'd find it funny and realistic if he looked like a deer in headlights (and you'll find its my favorite expression to give him in my own fanart).

The pairing has a hint of "doomed to snuff out." Something worth exploring even outside of the ship is Jax's habit of compartmentalizing people into imaginary boxes." Because of it he will objectify even his friends as though they are lab experiments.
