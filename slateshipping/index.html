What is Slateship?

"NortLuchi/LuchiNort (ノトルキnotoruki)" refers to the fan pairing between two playable characters from Identity V: Luchino Diruse, a giant bloodthirsty lizard man and Norton Campbell, a prospector.

I am a NortLuchi monoshipper and I've spent my entire involvement in the Identity V fandom creating sculpts and paintings for this pairing because its a relationship I strive to have. If your mind is open, please keep reading!
